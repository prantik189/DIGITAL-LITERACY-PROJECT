Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students

Do's:

1) Use the privacy settings on Social Media to control who can see your Social Media posts.

2) Take a moment to think before you post something on Social Media. Consider how it may affect your future.

3) Only share information on Social Media that you know is accurate and verified.

4) Be respectful and polite when you comment on Social Media or send messages on Social Media.

%) Maintain an image on Social Media especially on platforms like LinkedIn.

Dont'ts:

1) Do not share personal or sensitive information like your address or phone number on Social Media.

2) Do not engage in cyberbullying. Get into arguments with people on Social Media.

3) Do not post things on Social Media that're offensive or inappropriate or illegal.

4) Do not believe everything you read on Social Media. Do not spread fake news, on Social Media without checking the facts.

5) Do not spend much time on Social Media because it can affect your studies or your health.
